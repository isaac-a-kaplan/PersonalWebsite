Bycicle Crank Breaking Point Design

Mechanics II: 3D Design (24-262)

Spring 2024

Designed a memeber to be installed as a bycicle crank to break at A force of 12 Newtons to prevent lower limb injuries during intense breaking manuvers. A hybrid material approach was taken to allow for the fast design iteration, testing, and reuse of parts. For the real world application, after breaking, only the acrylic bar slot would have to be replaced, and the force at which it would break could be selected by the consumer.

Technologies: CAD (solidworks), 3D printing
